Benjamin Corcoran

Electrical & Computer Engineering Student

It has never been easy for me to stick with one hobby or interest. So, throughout my childhood, I bounced from one hobby to the next with an "obsessive" behavior, according to my parents and peers. Most say mastering requires specialization, however, I disagree. Although I was unaware of how the knowledge I gained from each endeavor would impact me in the future, I soon learned that this breadth of knowledge would allow me to make connections between ideas that were not obvious to those around me. After I began college, I discovered the one thing that all of my interests had in common: creating. This realization led me to find my true passion in the field of computer engineering, where I could combine my love for art and design with mathematics to create valuable solutions for everyday problems. I believe my obsessive nature and my diverse background will benefit me in this constantly evolving field, and, most importantly, I am eager to learn and create new relationships along the way.
